About Duby LLC

Founded in 2015, Duby LLC emerged from a vision to revolutionize the aluminum fabrication industry. Our founders, with over 50 combined years of welding and materials engineering expertise, recognized a critical gap in the market: the need for a comprehensive, integrated aluminum solutions provider. What started as a small operation with a dedicated team of craftsmen has evolved into a trusted partner for businesses across aerospace, automotive, construction, and industrial sectors. We've grown by staying true to our founding principles: uncompromising quality, customer-centric service, and continuous innovation. Today, we proudly serve hundreds of satisfied clients worldwide, each project a testament to our commitment to excellence.

At our core, we believe that exceptional aluminum solutions require more than technical expertise—they demand a partnership built on trust, transparency, and mutual success. Our mission is to empower businesses by providing end-to-end aluminum solutions that exceed expectations and drive measurable results. We're committed to environmental sustainability, supporting our communities, and fostering a workplace culture that attracts and retains the industry's finest talent. Our core values—integrity, excellence, innovation, and customer focus—guide every decision we make and every project we undertake. We don't just deliver products; we deliver partnerships that create lasting value.

We specialize in sourcing a comprehensive range of aluminum alloys including 6061-T6, 7075-T73, 5083-H321, and 2024-T4, among many others. Our network of verified suppliers allows us to access both standard commercial alloys and specialized compositions tailored to your specific application requirements. Whether you need alloys for aerospace, automotive, marine, or industrial applications, we can source materials that meet or exceed your specifications and include proper certifications and documentation.

Our post-weld treatment services include heat treatment for strength optimization, stress relief processes to eliminate residual stresses, surface finishing for corrosion resistance and aesthetics, and specialized treatments for specific alloy requirements. Each treatment protocol is precisely calibrated to your material specifications and application demands. Our certified engineers design custom treatment plans that enhance structural integrity, improve mechanical properties, and extend the service life of your aluminum components. We maintain complete documentation for full traceability and compliance verification, ensuring your welds meet or exceed all applicable standards and requirements.
